What is... Property World?


Property World is a real estate project, created by Leah Cheong, Hung Nguyen and Abel Tebeje as a way to allow both professional real estate agents and normal people to be able to upload their properties to sell easily without troubles in navigating the platform.

Our Company


Property World.Inc is a real estate company locates at Atlanta, specialized in real estate secured online marketplace. For 2 weeks, Property World has been the leading platform for 2 weeks in terms of customer services, safe experiences and innovation in an underrated side of the tech industry.

Our Services


Currently, we provide a platform for people who are looking to sell their properties to be able to add their properties to the market privately and name their own prices without any interferences from the government.
